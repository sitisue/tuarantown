EVENT OF THE DAY IN PEKAN TUARAN

Duang Festival (25-28 February 2024)

Throughout the year, Sabah entices visitors with a variety of events, from traditional festivals to wildlife adventures. In February, experience the Duang Festival (Feb 25-28), a vibrant cultural celebration of the Bajau Sama ethnic community in the Tuaran district, a gateway to rural destinations and hidden gems.
