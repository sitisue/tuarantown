THINGS TO DO IN TUARAN TOWN, SABAH

1. TAMU TUARAN SUNDAY MORNING MARKET

At Tamu Tuaran Sunday Morning Market, there are various sales that can be found including local delicacies for breakfast. There are also fresh fruits and vegetables sold by local people. Local handicrafts are also widely sold here. If you don't know, the Tuaran culture is famous for its beautiful hand-woven baskets that can be used as ornaments or souvenirs for family and friends.

2. LING-SAN PAGODA TOWER

Ling-San Pagoda Tower is a place of worship for the Chinese community, and the Buddhist religion. This temple is main landmark and main tourist attraction in Tuaran town. This beautiful temple with nine storey pagoda is modelled from the ancient chinese architecture. In addition, the pagoda tower is also an attraction for the tourists.

3. CLOCK TOWER TUARAN TOWN

For your information, the Clock Tower is a landmark of Tuaran Town. The old clock with a concrete structure over 50 years old stands tall and beautiful in Kent Street, Tuaran Town.

4. NULUH LAPAI HILL

Head out on this 1.8-km out-and-back trail near Tuaran, Sabah. Generally considered a moderately challenging route, it takes an average of 50 min to complete. This is a popular trail for hiking, running, and walking, but the tourists can still enjoy some solitude during quieter times of day. This trail starts at Jalan Sulaiman and will take you to Nuluh Lapai, where there are beautiful scenery.
